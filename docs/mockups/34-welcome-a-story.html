Your data flows in

Watch runs, weigh-ins, sleep, VO₂max — synced automatically from Apple Health and Withings. Nothing to type, nothing leaves your server.

Every Sunday it reviews the week — every set, every run, every trend — and writes next week's plan with reasons attached.
