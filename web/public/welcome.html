FORGE.

A coach that has actually read your training log.

Forge is a self-hosted training app with an agent coach. Your workouts, weigh-ins and runs flow in from Apple Health and Withings — every Sunday the coach reads all of it and proposes your next week. You approve it from your phone.

Private instance, two seats — or hit “Try the demo” on the sign-in screen.
The coach

Your week, proposed — never imposed

The coach cites your actual numbers, explains every change, and waits for your approval. Niggles shape what it prescribes; cool-downs are never dropped.

How did my squat move this block?
Cleanly — 8/8/8 at 75 kg twice, RPE 7. Next week takes it to 77.5 kg for the first time, and the easy run stretches to 50 min. The proposal's on your Today screen.
Cardio

Runs sync themselves

Finish a Watch run and it lands here matched to the plan: route, heart-rate curve, time in zone. No map tiles, no third parties — your GPS never leaves your server.

Progress

Strength you can see

Estimated 1RM per lift, bodyweight against goal, VO₂max trend, weekly Zone-2 minutes — computed from what you actually logged, never from wishful thinking.

In the gym

Fast to log, smart under time pressure

Tell it you've got 40 minutes and it trims accessories — never the main lift. Plate math from your own inventory, warm-up ramps, rest timers, offline-tolerant logging.
